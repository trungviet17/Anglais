Studying, now more fun with AI

ANGLaiS is a free web application, desinged to aid in language studying. 
With the assistant of AI, we plan to create a convenient and non-stressing environment
 that can help the majority of people to learn any type of languge that they wish to.

How it works

Create your own studysets of any languages you want.
Review your memory by remembering your own collection of vocabulary.
Have AI look through your essay assignment to evaluate your work

We provide these following features

C.R.R - Create.Review.Repeat

Create your own collection of vocabulary to learn them in the form of flip cards, or try out one of our default sets

Carolina - AI literature reviewer

Conveniently let Carolina - Miss AI check your essays to find grammatical errors, or evaluate overall quality

Mastermind - Text to Image AI

A very fun add-on of a finetuned AI model that might interest you
